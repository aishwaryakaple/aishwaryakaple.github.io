About

Hello, I am Aishwarya. I am currently an R&D intern at the UNESCO International Institute for Educational Planning (IIEP-UNESCO)

Later this Spring, I will graduate with a Masters in International Educational Development from the University of Pennsylvania.

My focus lies in building effective, contextual, and sustainable systems in education that contribute towards the development of the society as a whole.
